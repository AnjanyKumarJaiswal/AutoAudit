We act as a Data Processor under GDPR. Customer data is hosted on AWS in the us-east-1 (Virginia) and eu-central-1 (Frankfurt) regions. We do not sell personal data to third parties. Data deletion requests are processed within 30 days of verification. All personal data is pseudonymized at the application layer. We have appointed a Data Protection Officer (DPO) who can be reached at dpo@finguard.io. Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) are available upon request for all enterprise customers. We support data portability through our API, allowing customers to export their data in standard formats (JSON, CSV). Cross-border data transfers are governed by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission.
