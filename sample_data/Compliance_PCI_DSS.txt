As a Level 1 Service Provider, FinGuard undergoes annual onsite SOC 2 Type II and PCI DSS audits. We perform quarterly external vulnerability scans and annual penetration testing by independent third-party security firms. All audit logs are retained for a minimum of 365 days. Our PCI DSS compliance covers all 12 requirements including maintaining a secure network, protecting cardholder data, maintaining a vulnerability management program, implementing strong access control measures, regularly monitoring and testing networks, and maintaining an information security policy. We hold current certifications for PCI DSS v4.0, SOC 2 Type II, and ISO 27001:2022.
