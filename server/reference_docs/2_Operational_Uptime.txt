FinGuard guarantees 99.99% monthly uptime for our core API endpoints. We utilize a multi-region, active-active deployment strategy across AWS and GCP to ensure high availability. Our RTO (Recovery Time Objective) is 4 hours, and RPO (Recovery Point Objective) is 15 minutes. Automated failover is configured between primary and secondary regions with health checks every 30 seconds. We maintain real-time monitoring using Datadog and PagerDuty with escalation policies for P1 incidents. Our average incident response time is under 15 minutes for critical severity issues. Planned maintenance windows are communicated 72 hours in advance and are typically scheduled during off-peak hours (2:00 AM - 4:00 AM UTC).
